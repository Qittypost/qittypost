1. Introduction

Who are we

QittyPostBot is a commercial project created to expand opportunities and simplify the creation of posts in Telegram channels.

Why the privacy policy was created

The privacy policy was created to outline how our organization collects, uses, stores, and protects users' personal information. It ensures transparency and builds trust with users by informing them about their rights and how their data is handled. Another reason for creating a privacy policy is to comply with Telegram's requirements regarding data handling and user privacy. Additionally, a privacy policy helps organizations meet legal requirements and regulations related to data protection.

2. What information we collect

Automatically Collected Information:

  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your account.
  • Language Preference: The language setting you use in Telegram.
  • Messages: Any messages sent between you and the bot, including commands and responses.

Voluntary Information:

  • Contact Information: Any contact details you provide (e.g., email address, phone number) for communication purposes.
  • Payment Information: If applicable, any payment-related data necessary for processing transactions (note: sensitive payment details should be handled securely and may be processed through a third-party payment processor).
  • Additional Data: Any other information you choose to share with us while using the bot, such as feedback, preferences, or responses to surveys.

3. How we use your data

  • To process orders and payments: Your data is used to handle transactions, ensuring accurate billing and order processing.
  • To respond to user requests: We use your data to answer any inquiries or fulfill requests made through the bot.
  • To provide and improve services: Your data helps us enhance the functionality of the bot and tailor it to your needs based on usage patterns.
  • For marketing purposes: If you have provided consent, we may use your data to send newsletters, promotions, or other relevant marketing materials.

4. Data storage and protection

All databases are stored on a remote server. Access to the databases is not granted to third parties, and only the authors of the service have access to them. Some data, such as Telegram user IDs, are stored permanently, while other data may be deleted over time.

5. Do we share your data with third parties

Your data may be shared with third parties solely for the purpose of processing payments and analytics. This includes transferring data to payment system services and platforms such as Google Analytics. These services help us enhance our operations and provide better service to our users.
We may share your data with government authorities, such as the police or other law enforcement agencies, in the event of a request or demand related to the investigation of criminal or fraudulent activities. This may be done to assist in the identification of criminals or to protect our users from unlawful actions.

6. Your rights

Users have the right to modify certain data, such as connected channels and users who have access rights to these channels. Additionally, users can completely delete all their data from our service simply by deleting their account. To do this, you need to enter the command /account or navigate to Settings -> My Account -> Delete Account.

7. Updates to the Privacy Policy

The privacy policy may be updated at any time, but it will not be updated very frequently. Users will be informed of any new privacy policy through our Telegram channel and via broadcast messages in the bot.
